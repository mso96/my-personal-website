Menemen is one of those dishes that feels too simple until it is perfect.

You need tomatoes, green peppers, eggs, olive oil, salt, black pepper, and maybe onion if you are brave enough to enter that debate.

How I like it:

1. Cook chopped peppers slowly in olive oil.
2. Add tomatoes and let them collapse into a sauce.
3. Season properly.
4. Add eggs and stir gently.
5. Stop before it turns dry.

Eat it with bread straight from the pan. That is not a serving suggestion. That is the point.
